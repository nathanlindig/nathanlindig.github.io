About

Character Technical Artist — VR · Social · Real-time — Pipeline · Tools · Art

I'm a Character Technical Artist wired to see the world through the eyes of an artist — but trained to think through problems like an engineer. Where an artist focuses on the result they want to create, I'm thinking about how to approach the problem and how to make the process better, easier, faster, and more enjoyable for everyone involved.

My background spans every aspect of CG production — modeling, texturing, rigging, shading, pipeline development, and tooling — across VR, social platforms, and real-time character systems. That breadth gives me a unique ability to anticipate how a decision in one part of a pipeline ripples downstream, and to design solutions with that full context in mind.

I don't settle for the tools in front of me. I'm always looking for new techniques and better approaches — not just to grow my own capabilities, but to expand what's possible for the teams I serve. That same instinct extends to the people I work with: I can sit with an engineer and explain how an artist sees a problem, and I can sit with an artist and help them understand why constraints exist. Translation between disciplines — in both directions — is something I do naturally, and it's one of the things I value most about the work.
